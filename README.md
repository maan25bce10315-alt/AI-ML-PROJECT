## **🔎 Overview**

The **Heart Disease Prediction System** focuses on early detection of cardiovascular diseases, which are one of the leading causes of death worldwide.

By leveraging **Machine Learning** techniques such as **Logistic Regression**, the system analyzes patient health parameters (like age, cholesterol, blood pressure, etc.) to predict whether a person is at risk of heart disease.

This automated prediction system helps in faster diagnosis and supports medical decision-making with an accuracy of up to **85–90%**, depending on the dataset used.

---

## **🚀 Key Features**

🐍 **Machine Learning Logic**

* **Binary Classification:** Predicts whether a patient has heart disease (1) or not (0).  
* **Logistic Regression Model:** Efficient and widely used algorithm for medical classification tasks.  
* **Model Evaluation:** Accuracy scores for both training and testing datasets.

📊 **Data Processing**

* **Data Cleaning:** Handles missing or inconsistent values.  
* **Feature Scaling:** Normalizes input features.  
* **Train-Test Split:** Ensures proper model evaluation.

🧠 **Health Parameters Used**

* Age  
* Sex  
* Chest Pain Type  
* Resting Blood Pressure  
* Cholesterol Level  
* Fasting Blood Sugar  
* Maximum Heart Rate  
* Exercise-Induced Angina
